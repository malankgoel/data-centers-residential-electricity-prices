
TIME-VARYING ENRICHMENT — HEADLINE RESULTS
==========================================
Of 1550 OpenStreetMap data center records:
  - 519 have VERIFIED opening years
    (from OSM tags, Wikidata, Wikipedia operator/city pages).
  - 446 imputed from operator median.
  - 208 imputed from state median.
  - 377 imputed from global empirical median.

The state-year panel now carries CUMULATIVE-BY-YEAR data center counts
rather than a fixed pre-2022 cross-section. This is what enters the
new time-varying DiD specifications.
